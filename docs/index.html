Key Findings

Employment (Q1)

  • NYC Total Nonfarm (Feb 2026): 4,791,000 — declined 0.82% year-over-year
  • Largest YoY decline: Health Care & Social Assistance (−24,200 jobs, −2.32%), driven by CDPAP policy reform rather than economic contraction
  • Largest YoY gain: Transportation & Warehousing (+5,400, +5.21%), consistent with e-commerce logistics expansion
  • 5-year comparison: Baseline (Feb 2021) is COVID-distorted, making percentage changes reflect recovery rather than organic growth

311 Service Requests (Q2)

  • Total complaints: 198,158 — all NYPD (HPD returned zero matching rows)
  • Illegal Parking: 86,493 (44%) — nearly as many as all noise combined
  • Noise - Residential: 75,543 (38%) — single largest complaint type
  • Research question: Do lower-income neighborhoods generate more complaints per capita, controlling for population density?

Data: NYS DOL CES · NYC 311 OpenData
